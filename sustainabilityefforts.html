Our Sustainability Efforts

We have the compromise to help build a greener world with food.

Reducing Plastic

We have reduce 30% of plastic bags in our store. By offering reusable bags and using less harmful materials in our packaging.

Local Farming

We partner with local farmers to reduce food miles, additionally with this you get fresh and quality food on your table .

Carbon Neutral Goals

With some electric vehicles already on the road we plan to fully change to carbon neutral delivery by 2030.
